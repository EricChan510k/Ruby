Overview

This model reresents the doctor-patient relationship.

When a doctor adds ("tags") a patient, they're added to the "tagged" state When a doctor actually prescribes to a patient, they are sent an email invitation and this record is moved to the "invited" state When a patient logs in and confirms, the record is converted to the "active" state
